Hi, I'm Mattia Giurato.

I'm an AOCS & GNC Specialist at D-Orbit, working on attitude and orbit control for the ION Satellite Carrier programme. Previously Co-Founder and CTO of ANT-X, a UAV platform company spun off from Politecnico di Milano. Following you can find further information about me.

About Me

I was born in Milan (Italy), in 1991. I received the B.Sc. and the M.Sc. degrees in Automation and Control Engineering (in 2013 and 2015 respectively) from Politecnico di Milano, and my Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering (advisor: Prof. M. Lovera) in January 2020. After a post-doctoral research fellowship and co-founding ANT-X — a PoliMi spin-off producing educational UAV platforms for universities worldwide — I joined D-Orbit as AOCS & GNC Specialist, where I lead the attitude and orbit control subsystem for the ION Satellite Carrier programme. My technical interests span GNC for spacecraft and UAVs, model-based design, system identification, and robust control.

Work

D-Orbit s.p.a.

AOCS & GNC Specialist (Subsystem Leader) • June 2024 - Present

D-Orbit provides space logistics and satellite transportation solutions. As AOCS Subsystem Leader for the ION Satellite Carrier programme:

  • • Led development of AOCS software: attitude and orbit GNC algorithms and FDIR logic, compliant with ECSS and Savoir guidelines; maintained the MATLAB/Simulink model-based design environment with code generation for FSW delivery.
  • • Executed the full V&V pyramid: MIL, SIL, PIL, and HIL campaigns.
  • • Contributed to the ION-mk3 next-generation satellite: requirements flowdown, subsystem budgets, and system-level analyses.
  • • Supported ground-segment operations for LEO missions: telemetry monitoring, anomaly investigation, and mission analysis.

ANT-X s.r.l.

Co-Founder and CTO • February 2020 - June 2024

ANT-X is a PoliMi spin-off producing educational UAV platforms and research infrastructure for universities and laboratories worldwide. As CTO, I held full technical ownership across a team of 6:

  • • Designed and sized multirotor platforms from scratch based on Pixhawk/PX4 architecture; customized the PX4 firmware to enable seamless deployment of MATLAB/Simulink code-generated GNC logic.
  • • Integrated multi-environment localisation: outdoor GPS and indoor motion-capture and stereo-vision with an onboard companion computer for high-level autonomy.
  • • Delivered dozens of platforms annually to university research labs across Europe and the USA.
